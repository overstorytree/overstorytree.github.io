What We Do

Our Services

From routine maintenance to hazardous removals, every job is handled with technical precision and care for your property.

Tree Removal

Safe, controlled removal of trees of any size—from tight suburban lots to large canopy specimens. We use systematic rigging and sectional dismantling to protect your home, lawn, and neighboring plants. All debris hauled away.

Pruning & Canopy Management

Structural pruning, crown thinning, deadwood removal, and canopy lifting performed to ISA best practices. Promotes long-term tree health, improves light penetration, and reduces wind resistance and failure risk.

Tree Risk Assessment

Formal evaluation of structural integrity, root zone health, and site conditions. We provide a clear written report with risk ratings and recommended actions—giving you the information you need to make confident decisions.

Storm Damage Response

Rapid response to storm-damaged, split, or hanging limbs threatening your home or safety. We stabilize hazardous situations, remove debris, and work to preserve trees wherever viable. Available for urgent situations.

Stump Grinding

Complete stump removal using professional grinding equipment. We grind below grade so you can replant, sod, or build over the area. Grindings raked out or hauled away—your choice. No unsightly remnants left behind.

Contract Climbing

Experienced climbers available to other tree companies and operations on a contract basis. Whether you need an extra hand on a large job, a technical rigging specialist, or coverage during busy season, we provide skilled, safety-conscious climbers who show up prepared and work seamlessly with your crew.

Why Overstory

The Standard We Hold Ourselves To

Tree work done right takes more than a chainsaw and a truck. It takes trained eyes, disciplined process, and genuine respect for your property.

  • Rigorous Safety Standards

    Every job starts with a hazard assessment. We follow ANSI Z133 safety practices and never cut corners under a hazardous canopy.

  • Skilled Climbing & Rigging

    Our climbers are trained in technical rigging for controlled lowering—so trees come down exactly where we plan, not where gravity decides.

  • Respect for Your Property

    We protect your lawn, beds, and structures. Plywood and mats are used when needed. We treat your yard like we'd treat our own.

  • Clear Communication, Every Time

    You'll always know who's coming, when they'll arrive, and exactly what work is planned. Written estimates before work begins—always.

  • Thorough Cleanup Included

    Branches, brush, chips, and debris are fully cleared and hauled. We blow down hardscapes and rake before we leave. Clean job site—every time.
